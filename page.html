Tarlac State University

The Main Campus

The Heart and Body of TSU, The Main Campus Located at San Roque, Tarlac City, Serves as the base of operations for the administration of the university. Also housing some of the most outstanding College and fantastic Faculties.

The TSU Gymnasium at the Main Campus has a 2,000-seating capacity and is used for big university-wide events and activities, both academic and non-academic in nature, like graduation, socializations, intramurals, pageants, among others.
